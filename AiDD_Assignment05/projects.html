Pothole Detection System

Springer Publication | 2021-2022

The Challenge: India's road conditions cause thousands of accidents annually. Traditional inspection methods are slow and expensive. What if AI could spot dangerous potholes in real-time, protecting drivers and enabling smarter infrastructure investment?

What I Built: A computer vision system that turns dashcam footage into actionable safety data. Think of it as giving vehicles a sixth sense for road hazards—detecting potholes at highway speeds with enough accuracy for real deployment.

Results & Recognition

  • 82% detection accuracy with YOLOv5m—deployment-ready for real-world ADAS systems
  • Built and annotated 1,995-image dataset capturing India's diverse road conditions
  • Optimized for real-time processing through strategic anchor tuning and augmentation
  • Published in Springer conference proceedings—peer-validated research

Technical Deep Dive

Benchmarked YOLOv5 variants against Faster-RCNN with ResNet101 backbone to find the sweet spot between accuracy and speed. Implemented aggressive data augmentation to handle India's challenging lighting and weather conditions. The result? A system fast enough for highway speeds, accurate enough for driver safety.

Live-in-Labs Automation Initiative

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham | 2020-2021

The Mission: Rural artisans in Byse were losing to factory-made alternatives. Their eco-friendly areca-leaf plates were perfect for sustainability-conscious markets, but handcrafting limited production. How could we scale without losing the human touch?

What We Created: Not a replacement for artisans, but a multiplication of their skills. Our semi-automatic system let them triple output while keeping quality control in human hands. This wasn't about efficiency for efficiency's sake—it was about creating economic opportunity without destroying tradition.

Human Impact

  • Led 8-person cross-disciplinary team—engineers, designers, and community members as co-creators
  • 3× production increase without sacrificing the quality that made these plates special
  • Deployed in Byse community—turned users into micro-entrepreneurs, not factory workers
  • Created sustainable documentation—playbooks and maintenance guides for long-term independence

The Build Process

Started with field research—actually living with artisans to understand their workflow. Rapidly prototyped mechanical components that complemented human skill rather than replaced it. Documented everything so local craftspeople could maintain and replicate the system without external dependency. The goal was empowerment, not just efficiency.

Schlumberger | 2023-2024

The Stakes: Migrating enterprise SAP systems is where careers end. One bad data conversion, and you're explaining million-dollar discrepancies to executives. Zero room for error, but manual reviews don't scale. How do you move fast while sleeping at night?

My Framework: I built the safety net that made speed possible. A validation system so comprehensive that errors couldn't slip through, so automated that humans focused on judgment calls, not tedious checks. The result? Zero compliance issues across the entire migration.

Mission-Critical Outcomes

  • 100% compliance maintained—not a single governance violation across thousands of data conversions
  • 60% reduction in manual review time through intelligent automation
  • Complete audit trails—every change tracked, every decision documented, rollback always possible
  • Zero operational disruption—critical business processes never skipped a beat

The philosophy? Automate the tedious, empower the thoughtful, document everything.
